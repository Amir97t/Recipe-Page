Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beeaten egg cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Aproximatly 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings :cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meats, herb

Instruction

  • Beat the eggs:In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  • Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oill.
  • Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and rubbling, pour in the eggs. Till the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  • Add filling (optinal): When the eggs begin to seat the edges but are still slightly runny in the midlle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  • Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if nedded.
